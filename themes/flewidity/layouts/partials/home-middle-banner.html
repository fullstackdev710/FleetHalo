Discover the Fleet Halo Advantage

FleetHalo offers comprehensive telematics solutions suitable for all business sizes. It provides historical and real-time actionable trip data that helps manage inventory, vehicles and drivers. Drivers can log in and self-monitor work hours, fatigue levels and more from a single dashboard.

Its key modules include fleet reports, routing and dispatch, driver and vehicle tracking, and asset tracking and management.

  • Complete visibility of workforce, assets, and costs
  • Dispatching, navigation and route optimization
  • Collision detection & notification, driver safety scorecard
  • High-performance GPS technology
  • Advanced dashboard reports
  • Open platform for easy data integration
  • Marketplace Apps and Add-Ons
