Frequently asked questions

Trailer fleet management involves collecting, observing, and analyzing data associated with tow trucks and trailers. Through telematics devices and platforms, towing companies can monitor vehicle health, pinpoint locations, and understand usage trends. Fleet managers and dispatchers have the ability to oversee driving safety scorecards, determine the nearest vehicle for service calls, and observe driving behaviors such as speeding, harsh braking, and excessive idling. This information can be consolidated for comprehensive analysis, aiding in identifying both vehicle maintenance needs and opportunities for driver training.

FleetHalo GO and MyFleetHalo also enable tracking of the location, usage, condition, and maintenance requirements of remote trailer enclosures.

The Flex TT600, a solar-powered asset tracker, employs GPS technology to offer insights into asset utilization and location through the MyGeotab platform. It measures trailer utilization, indicating the proportion of time a trailer is actively employed and generating revenue for your business.

By monitoring utilization rates of trailers, fleet managers can:

  • Strategically plan trailer capacity for seasonal demand fluctuations.
  • Assess the optimal mix of owned versus rented trailers.
  • Make informed decisions regarding the acquisition of new trailers based on actual usage data.

The Flex TT600, a solar-powered asset tracker available on the Geotab Marketplace, gathers data and transmits it through GPS to the MyGeotab platform. This system enables the management of trailer utilization, location, and maintenance data, assisting in making informed decisions about when trailers require repair or replacement.

FleetHalo devices are tailored for a broad spectrum of vehicles, encompassing modern light, medium, heavy, and heavy-duty tow trucks, as well as flatbed vehicles. The FleetHalo RUGGED device, specifically designed for durability, is ideal for heavy-duty vehicles that require an external installation. Key features of these devices include:

  • Precise Location Tracking: Determine the exact location of tow trucks to dispatch the closest available driver efficiently.
  • Monitoring Vehicle Health and Maintenance: Keep tabs on the vehicle's condition and preemptively address maintenance needs.
  • Driver Behavior Oversight: Observe and analyze driving patterns including harsh braking, sharp turning, and speeding.
  • Optimized Routing and Fuel Tracking: Enhance route planning and monitor fuel usage for efficiency.
  • Geofencing Functionality: Ensure that drivers remain within their designated operational zones.

In addition, FleetHalo’s devices are capable of dispatching, routing, and monitoring a wide array of vehicles from heavy transport vehicles and light-duty trucks to passenger cars and SUVs.

The Flex TT60x, a solar-powered asset tracker, equips fleet managers with the ability to monitor their trailers' utilization rates. This feature helps in understanding the extent of trailer usage. Utilizing this data, fleet managers can make informed decisions about relocating trailers for optimal use or phasing out underperforming ones.

Additionally, the tracker's versatility extends to monitoring various assets like containers and generators. Key features of the Flex TT60x include:

  • Durable Design: It's built tough to withstand the rigors commonly faced by large trailers.
  • Extended Battery Life: The solar cell enhances the tracker's longevity, reducing maintenance needs.
  • Efficient Performance: The tracker not only helps in maintaining regular checks to prevent breakdowns but also in understanding how trailers are being utilized to their fullest potential.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: It provides continuous updates on asset status, thus improving customer service by keeping them informed about their goods.

In essence, the Flex TT60x is a vital tool for fleet managers, aiding in the effective management of trailers and other assets, ensuring they are efficiently used and maintained, and offering up-to-date information for enhanced operational decisions.
