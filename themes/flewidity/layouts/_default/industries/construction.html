Specialized telematics software for construction fleet management is tailored to the needs of fleets in the construction sector.

The core of this system is our robust, fully ruggedized telematics device, the GO9 RUGGED™, boasting IP68 and IP69K ratings and engineered to withstand extreme worksite conditions, including external installations. This device captures high-performance GPS data, integrated into the MyGeotab fleet management platform. Here, managers can oversee fuel consumption and idling patterns, track engine hours and Power Take-Off (PTO) usage, monitor real-time locations of fleet vehicles and assets, ensure Duty of Care compliance, and enhance driver safety through behavior dashboards and in-vehicle coaching.

By incorporating apps from the Geotab Marketplace, the software further aids in transitioning from reactive to proactive maintenance strategies. This is achieved by utilizing fault codes and engine hour data, which helps in reducing vehicle wear and tear while boosting overall vehicle utilization.

Construction sites are bustling environments with numerous job locations, a variety of assets, and active crews. The construction fleet management solutions offered by Geotab are designed to:

  • Boost productivity while keeping costs in check
  • Enhance billing accuracy
  • Ensure compliance with workplace regulations
  • Elevate the safety of drivers and worksites
  • Provide real-time tracking of construction and heavy equipment assets, offering improved oversight across different sites
  • Enhance vehicle utilization by shifting from a reactive to a proactive maintenance approach
  • Strengthen the security of assets
